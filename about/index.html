About AUCC Atlanta

AUCC exists to support Ukrainians through education, networking, and business connections that help members build long-term financial stability.

Purpose

Build financial independence

AUCC provides practical opportunities to grow skills, confidence, and personal stability through financial education and peer support.

Values

How we work

Respect: inclusive and supportive community.
Integrity: honest, responsible communication.
Learning: practical, actionable education.

Governance

Nonprofit and volunteer-led

The organization is operated by board leadership and committees, with published governance materials available to members.
